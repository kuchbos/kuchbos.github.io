Cultural Evolution of the Arts

I am a researcher studying cultural evolution of the arts. I discover and explain long-term patterns in the history of books, movies, songs, video games, and other art forms. My goal is to help building a theory-driven quantitative history of culture. For this, I analyze large digital libraries and artistic datasets (such as IMDb or Spotify), use statistical models and text mining.
